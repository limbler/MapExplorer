about:

MapExplorer provides a simple format to create a journey through a 2D world. How you choose to use it is up to you. Will you create a visual world with many nooks and crannies to explore? Will you use it to tell a story, like a DIY adventure novel? Will you use it to craft funny little comics to share with your friends? Or will you come up with something entirely new and unexpected?

Check out this Sample Project to see how it works

instructions:

Start your new map with a single image.

Click the "Create Selection" button, then click on your image to draw a rectangle around an area that you want to be "explored" further. For instance, if your image is of a house, you may wish to select the front door. When you are satisfied with your selection, click the "Save Selection" button.

You will be prompted to upload a new image for this selection. When the user clicks on this selection in the future, they will be taken to this new image. In the house example, this next image may be of the inside of the house just past the door. Note that each image can have multiple selections.

And that's it! Enjoy!
